我们是谁？ Who We Are
WHO WE ARE

我们是异味游戏同好会（Weird Games Club）。这是一个由二人组成的独立游戏厂牌，目的是发掘那些拥有作者性、邪典、怪诞和只为特定受众制作的游戏，将这些异味宝石带给真正热爱它们的人。

We are the Weird Games Club, a two-person indie outfit hunting for the cult, the surreal, and the beautifully bizarre. We believe that some games are only meant for a chosen few. Our job? To find those eccentric masterpieces and deliver them to the players who live for the weird.
