我们是谁？ Who We Are
WHO WE ARE

我们是异味游戏同好会（Weird Games Club）——一个由二人组成的独立游戏厂牌。我们发掘那些拥有作者性、邪典、怪诞、只为特定受众制作的游戏，把它们带给真正热爱异味的人。

我们也会以发行方身份，挑选、上架并推广选中的异味独立游戏；同时提供本地化与宣传合作。厂牌履历见「合作游戏」：野菌计划、本地化与推广、同好会发行。

We are the Weird Games Club — a two-person indie label hunting for the cult, the surreal, and the beautifully bizarre. Some games are only meant for a chosen few; our job is to find them and put them in the right hands.

We also publish selected weird indies, and partner on localization and promotion. See our catalog under Supported Games: Mushroom Land, Loc & Promo, and Published by Us.

异味雷区 Disclaimer & Boundaries
DISCLAIMER & BOUNDARIES
⚖️ 官方版权与合法合作声明
⚖️ Official Copyright & Legitimacy Notice
异味游戏同好会的本地化与发行相关合作均为与游戏开发商/发行方正式洽谈的合法商业行为。我们不提供任何非官方的民间汉化补丁、破解包或私下分发渠道。经由我们翻译或发行的作品，请通过 Steam、Switch 等官方渠道支持正版。
Localization and publishing collaborations under the Weird Games Club are formal, authorized business arrangements with developers and rights holders. We do not produce unofficial fan translations, cracks, or illicit downloads. Please support titles via official storefronts such as Steam and Nintendo Switch.
📢 社交媒体言论独立性声明
📢 Social Media Disclaimer
异味游戏同好会在社媒平台的官方账号发布的所有内容，仅代表同好会团队自身的学术观点与创作立场。除非明确标注"受官方委托发布"，否则我们的任何言论均不代表游戏原作开发商、发行商的官方商业态度与最终决策。
Everything we post on our social media channels reflects only the personal tastes, creative chaos, and academic opinions of our own little club. Unless a post is crystal-clear marked as "Officially Commissioned", our rants and raves do not represent the commercial mindset or final corporate decisions of the original developers or publishers. We speak for ourselves, and they speak for them.
🚫 拒绝为"非本地化引发的舆论风波"背锅
🚫 Not Our Circus, Not Our Monkeys
我们深知每款独立游戏都承载着作者强烈的个人表达。同好会仅对"文本翻译的准确性与艺术性"，以及我们作为发行方明确承诺的事项负责。若游戏原作者、其他发行商因其自身的政治立场、言论争议、运营策略或跳票行为引发任何社媒舆论风波，均与异味游戏同好会无关。团队拒绝接受任何形式的连带舆论网暴与无端指责。
We deeply respect that every indie game carries the fierce personal expression of its author. Our responsibility covers translation quality and whatever we expressly commit to as a publisher. Should original creators or other publishers face backlash over their own politics, statements, ops, or delays — the Weird Games Club remains unassociated. Zero tolerance for groundless pile-ons aimed at us by association.
