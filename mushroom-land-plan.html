吃下蘑菇，梦见死亡 Cibus deorum
WHAT IS IT

狂气的月光下，菌丝悄然蔓延，少女与哲人经历的微不足道的挫折化作养料，最终繁衍出一片世界末日的绝景……

野菌计划，是我们挑选觉得有异味的独立游戏，把它们当成蘑菇一样孵化的计划。撒上养料，等它破土——目的是带给玩家挥之不去的梦魇。要来跟我们一起召唤世界末日吗？

Under the frantic moonlight, mycelium silently creeps. Whether a commonplace girl or a great philosopher, their most trivial setbacks turn into nourishment, ultimately hatching a sublime spectacle of the apocalypse...

Mushroom Land is how we pick games that smell properly weird and incubate them like mushrooms — feed them, wait for them to break soil, leave you with a lingering nightmare. Will you join us in summoning the end of the world?

02
野菌是怎么破土而出的？ How does a mushroom break soil?
THE SPROUT

我们挑选自己觉得够异味、却未必已在简中社群里被充分看见的游戏，用同好会的力量帮它破土：曝光、本地化协助、必要时设计中文 logo、处理本地化技术问题等等。

野菌计划支持的游戏，不等于同好会挂名发行。有的我们只提供本地化与宣传；有的则会由同好会作为发行方上架。两条路径可以并存，以每款游戏的实际合作为准。

目前谈下来的几款野菌，多为免费游戏：我们免费做本地化，并在爱发电挂打赏。打赏回流，会用在同好会后续的发行游戏上——养料回林，再长下一朵菌。

每破土一朵野菌，主页侧边栏的计数也会更新。你可以点进收获野菌，也可以在异味林地里采摘。

We pick games weird enough for us that still need more light in the Simplified Chinese community — then help them break soil: visibility, localization support, Chinese logos when needed, technical help along the way.

Mushroom Land support does not always mean we are the publisher. Some titles get localization and promo only; others we publish under our own name. Both can happen — it depends on the deal for each game.

The first wave we have lined up are mostly free games: we localize them for free, and tip jars live on Afdian. Tips flow back into funding games we publish — compost for the next mushroom.

Each sprout updates the counter on the homepage sidebar. Harvest from there, or forage in The Humid Wilds.

想支持养料回流？可关注同好会爱发电： Want to feed the compost? Our Afdian: afdian.com/a/manwithouttalent

野菌与合作游戏、异味档案的关系 Mushroom Land, Supported Games & the Archives
RELATED PAGES

经野菌计划孵化的游戏，会出现在合作游戏页的「野菌计划」分区。若同款亦由同好会挂名发行，也会落在「同好会发行」里（以该作实际挂名为准）。

「本地化与推广」分区则是另一条线：面向付费商务合作的本地化与/或宣传推荐，与野菌计划的免费孵化不同。

采访、考据或资讯会写进异味档案。我们也会用文字和短视频，按季度汇报林地野菌的生长情况。

Titles incubated through Mushroom Land appear under Mushroom Land on Supported Games. If we also publish that title, it shows under Published by Us as well (based on the real publisher credit).

Localization & Promo is a separate lane: paid commercial loc and/or shout-outs — not the same as free Mushroom Land incubation.

Interviews and write-ups live in the Archives. We also share quarterly growth updates in text and short video.
